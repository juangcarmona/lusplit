FULL DESCRIPTION

Shared expenses, clear accounts.

LuSplit helps you track costs across any group — trips, homes, events, or anything else. Log each shared payment, see who owes what at a glance, and find the fastest way to settle up. No account needed. Works fully offline.

• Groups — Create groups for trips, homes, or any shared situation. Each group tracks its own expenses independently.

• Flexible Splits — Split equally, by amount, by percentage, or by custom weight. Preview the impact on balances before saving.

• Clear Balances — Every person's current balance, always up to date. No mental math required.

• Simple Settlement — LuSplit works out the fewest transfers needed to bring all balances to zero. No money moves through the app — it's a suggestion you act on directly.

• Full Expense History — Every expense in one place. Edit or delete past entries and balances recalculate instantly.

• Export Everything — Export your data as JSON, CSV, or PDF at any time.

• No Accounts Needed — No sign-up. No login. Works fully offline.

LuSplit handles nothing. All money moves directly between people. It makes shared costs simple.
