# LuSplit Brand

Version 1.0 - Foundational Identity

## Brand Name

**LuSplit**

Camel casing is mandatory.

- Never: `lusplit`
- Never: `LUSPLIT`
- Never: `Lu split`

## Origin Story

Born during a ski trip. Friends sharing expenses. Confusion avoided through clarity.

LuSplit exists to prevent small financial frictions from damaging relationships.

## Brand Purpose

Reduce social tension around shared money.

## Positioning

A calm shared-expense utility for families and friends.

- Not a fintech product
- Not a budgeting tool
- Not a banking app

It is a clarity tool.

## Brand Personality

- Calm
- Fair
- Honest
- Domestic
- Friendly but restrained
- Minimal
- Emotionally intelligent

Do not use:

- Aggressive fintech tone
- Gamification patterns
- Financial jargon-heavy language

## Brand Emotion Framework

When someone opens LuSplit, they should feel:

- Relief
- Clarity
- Calm resolution

LuSplit should feel like the friend who writes things down so nobody argues.

## Long-Term Brand Scalability

The icon is abstract enough to:

- Animate zipper opening → settling
- Extend into a product family
- Work as a favicon
- Print in monochrome

No seasonal snow dependency. Ski origin is story, not visual dependency.

## Brand Summary Statement

LuSplit is a calm utility that removes tension from shared expenses, born from friendship, built for fairness.
