# LuSplit Voice and Tone

Version 1.0 — Foundational Identity

## Voice Attributes

- Short
- Clear
- Friendly
- Direct
- Calm
- Restrained

## Lexicon Replacements

Use:

- `Debt` → `Owes`
- `Settlement` → `Settle up`
- `Transaction` → `Expense`
- `Participants` → `Friends`

Avoid:

- `Ledger`
- `Reconciliation`
- `Allocation`
- `Liability`

## Writing Guidance

- Prefer plain language over financial terminology
- Keep copy concise and emotionally neutral
- Emphasize clarity and fairness over urgency

## Example Phrases

- “You owe Alex €12”
- “All settled. Nice.”
